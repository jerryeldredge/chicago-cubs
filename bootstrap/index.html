About Me

Hi, I’m Jeremy, a student at the BYU Marriott School of Business pursuing a BS in Accounting and a Master of Accountancy. I grew up in Clemmons, North Carolina, and I’m currently based in Provo, Utah.

I spent two years as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Rosario, Argentina, where I learned to lead teams, manage logistics under pressure, and became fluent in Spanish. That experience taught me more about hard work and adaptability than anything else I’ve done.

Outside of school and work, I enjoy being with my fiance, Rylie, bowling, and yes, watching the Cubs
